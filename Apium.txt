if connecting phone
To enabled USB Debugging:
1. Open Settings on your phone
2. Search for "Build Number"/"Build version"/"OS version"/"Version Number".
3. Tap it about 7-8 times, till you see "You are now a developer!"
4. Then, go back to Settings and search for "Developer Options"
5. In there, turn on "USB debugging". On some phones, you might have to enable the following options also:
    - Look for "Install via USB" and enable it
    - Look for "USB Debugging (Security Settings)" and enable it
    - Look for "Disable Permission Monitoring" and enable it
    - Note: If you don't see the above options, ignore it.
6. Once the options are enabled, connect your phone to your system via USB cable.
7. Allow USB debugging on the device
8. You can also check by running the following commands in the termina:
    - cd AppData/Android/Sdk/platform-tools
    - .\adb devices
